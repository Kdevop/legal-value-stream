About this tool

This platform uses retrieval-augmented generation grounded on Employment Appeal Tribunal decisions to evaluate litigation risk in employment matters.

  • Unfair dismissal claims
  • Discrimination proceedings
  • Whistleblowing disputes
  • Redundancy and TUPE matters
  • Constructive dismissal

Risk Classifications

High: Significant litigation exposure. Immediate legal review recommended.

Medium: Moderate exposure. Further investigation advised.

Low: Limited litigation risk based on available precedents.

Legal Notice

This tool provides indicative risk assessment only and does not constitute legal advice. All assessments should be reviewed by a qualified solicitor before action is taken.
